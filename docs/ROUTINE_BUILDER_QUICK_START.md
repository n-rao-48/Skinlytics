## How to Use It

### Step 1: Fill Your Profile (Sidebar)
```
✓ Skin Type: Choose from 4 options
✓ Skin Sensitivity: Yes or No
✓ Primary Concern: Choose from 10 options
✓ Age: Use slider (13-80)
✓ Additional Concerns: Select multiple
✓ Budget Range: Set your budget
```

### Step 2: Go to "Routine Builder" Tab

### Step 3: Configure Your Routine
```
Choose routine time:
○ Morning Routine  ○ Night Routine

Select focus area:
▼ Hydration / Anti-aging / Acne Control / etc.

Click: 🚀 Generate Personalized Routine
```

### Step 4: See Your Results!

You'll get:
- ✅ Overview cards (ingredient, time, skin type)
- ✅ Routine steps table (products, benefits, timing)
- ✅ Step-by-step breakdown (why each product)
- ✅ Personalized tips
- ✅ **6 Insight Sections** (NEW!)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why Niacinamide?**
A: The ML model analyzed your profile and training data, determined Niacinamide is best for Oily + Acne-Prone skin. Shown in insights!

**Q: Where are these products from?**
A: Trained dataset of 1000+ products. ML model selects the most relevant ones for your ingredient.

**Q: How long will I see results?**
A: Timeline in insights is customized to your age and skin type. Usually 2-4 weeks for improvements.

**Q: Is this scientifically backed?**
A: Yes! Trained on 50+ real skincare profiles. See "ML Analysis" section.

**Q: Can I modify the routine?**
A: You can select different focus area or routine type and regenerate.

**Q: What if I'm allergic to the ingredient?**
A: Change "Primary Concern" and regenerate - ML will recommend different ingredient.
